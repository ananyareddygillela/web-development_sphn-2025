Mahavatar Narsimha is a 2024[b] Indian Hindi-language[13] animated epic devotional[14] action film directed by Ashwin Kumar in his directorial debut, written by Jayapurna Das, produced by Kleem Productions, and presented by Hombale Films.[15][16] The film is the first installment in the planned animated seven-part Mahavatar Cinematic Universe, based on the ten avatars of Vishnu
